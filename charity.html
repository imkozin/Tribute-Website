Identified as one of the most active people in the climate change movement, DiCaprio believes global warming is the world's "number-one environmental challenge". During his acceptance speech at the 88th Oscars Academy Awards, 2016 DiCaprio said:

Climate change is real, it is happening right now. It is the most urgent threat facing our entire species, and we need to work collectively together and stop procrastinating.

If you want to save our planet, please join us to help Leo!
